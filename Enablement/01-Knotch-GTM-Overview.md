# Knotch GTM Overview

## About Knotch

Knotch is a **content intelligence platform** that provides an independent standard for measuring content marketing ROI across owned and paid channels. We help enterprise and mid-market brands understand exactly which content drives business outcomes, enabling them to optimize marketing spend, improve content decisions, and prove marketing value to the enterprise.

### Core Products

**Knotch One** – The core SaaS platform for content intelligence, measurement, and attribution across owned and paid channels.

**ACE (Agentic Content Engine)** – AI-powered capabilities for content generation, optimization, and automation.

**K1** – Core measurement and analytics engine.

**Content Catalysts** – Pre-built content templates and frameworks aligned to buyer personas and revenue generation.

**JCI (Journey Content Intelligence)** – Journey mapping and content intelligence for go-to-market teams.

**KSC (Knotch Strategic Consulting)** – Professional services for implementation, custom integrations, and strategic measurement frameworks.

**AIQ** – AI-powered insights and recommendations engine.

**EQ/IQ** – Intelligence scoring modules for buyer intelligence and content performance.

## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

We target **Enterprise and upper-mid-market brands** with these characteristics:

- **Significant content marketing and digital media spend** – $1M+ annual investment in content creation, distribution, and paid media
- **Dedicated teams** – Organizations with dedicated content, brand, digital, or performance marketing departments
- **Digital-first approach** – Companies where digital channels drive material revenue or brand impact
- **Measurement maturity** – Organizations seeking to move beyond vanity metrics and establish ROI frameworks

### Target Verticals

- Financial Services (fintechs, payment platforms, wealth management)
- Consumer Packaged Goods (CPG) & Retail
- Technology / SaaS
- Healthcare (medtech, pharma, health systems)
- Media & Publishing

## GTM Team Structure

### Leadership

**Head of Sales** – Executive ownership of all revenue outcomes, sales strategy, and team development.

### Individual Contributors (AEs)

Account Executives manage the full sales process from prospecting through close. Each AE owns a portfolio of accounts and is responsible for new business generation, deal progression, and account expansion.

### SDR/BDR Function

Sales Development Representative and Business Development Representative teams handle initial prospect outreach, qualification, and IPM setting to feed the AE pipeline.

### Revenue Operations (GTM Evolved)

Dedicated ops team for:

- CRM architecture and data integrity
- Sales process optimization
- Forecasting and pipeline management
- Sales enablement and tooling
- Analytics and reporting

## Go-to-Market Tools & Data

### CRM

**HubSpot (Portal 44523005)** – Single source of truth for all customer and prospect data, pipeline management, and forecasting.

### Enrichment & Prospecting Stack

- **Apollo** – Contact reachability, email/phone data, and intent signals for prospecting
- **Clay** – Firmographic enrichment, technographic data, and custom data enrichment

### Meeting Intelligence & Note-Taking

- **Otter.ai** -- Primary meeting recording platform. Otter records every external call, generates a transcript, creates the meeting record in HubSpot, and attaches the notes automatically. This is the system of record for meeting activity.
- **Granola** -- Live AI note-taker that every seller runs during meetings. Granola supplements the Otter recording with real-time structured notes. After the meeting, sellers have one hour to review and refine their Granola notes into the standard meeting template. If the meeting matches a HubSpot contact, the notes are moved to a public Client Meetings folder and automatically attached to the contact, account, and deal records in HubSpot.
- **Claude** -- AI assistant connected to Granola, HubSpot, and other tools in the stack. Use Claude to ask questions about deals, accounts, meeting history, pipeline data, or to take actions across your tools. Every seller has access.

### Additional Capabilities

- **HubSpot Breeze** -- Automated basic firmographic enrichment on company creation
- **AI/Claude** -- Internal ICP scoring, persona classification, account intelligence summaries, and seller-facing assistant (connected to Granola, HubSpot, and enrichment tools)

## Sales Process Cadence

The typical deal progression follows our SPICED-based deal framework (see 03-Deal-Process-and-SPICED.md for full details):

1. **IPM** – Initial Product Meeting scheduled and held; opportunity confirmed
2. **Qualification (Stage 1)** – Pain and situation understood, SPICED foundation built
3. **Consensus (Stage 2)** – Buying group identified, solution fit validated, champion engaged
4. **Proposal + Business Case (Stage 3)** – Economic case prepared, proposal delivered
5. **Procurement (Stage 4)** – Legal, procurement, and final approval
6. **Closed Won / Closed Lost** – Deal terminal state
