Workstream 1

Revenue-Motion Architecture

  • Define qualification-gated pipeline stages aligned to SPICED (or hybrid methodology)
  • Document "CEO of the Deal" AE expectations: activity cadence, multi-threading rules, meeting targets
  • Reverse-engineer ICP from closed-won data and stakeholder interviews; build operational scoring rules
  • Design territory model and account-assignment logic
Workstream 2

CRM Implementation & Reporting

  • Hard-wire methodology into HubSpot: required fields, validation rules, buying-committee mapping
  • Build activity-to-outcome dashboards: outbound → meetings → pipeline → closed-won
  • Create manager views: AE performance vs. benchmarks, pipeline health, ICP-segment conversion
  • Document data model for downstream AI and forecasting tools
Workstream 3

Enrichment & Handoff Design

  • Configure firmographic and tech-stack enrichment mapped into CRM
  • Define lead scoring: ICP fit + engagement strength + activity history

Tech Stack Assessment & 90-Day Roadmap

  • Map current GTM stack, data flows, and friction points

Phase 2 — What Comes Next

Data-Forward, AI-Assisted, Admin-Light

With the foundation in place, Phase 2 takes admin work out of sales and equips reps for confident, data-driven meetings.

Advanced Data Enrichment Strategy

Layer intent data (Bombora, G2), technographic signals (BuiltWith, Clay), and web-traffic intelligence (Similarweb) into a unified enrichment pipeline. Score accounts on buying propensity, surface warm accounts before reps ask, and trigger automated sequences when signal thresholds are met.

Admin Out of Sales

Autofill CRM fields from email, calendar, and call transcripts. Surface tasks and follow-ups automatically. Reps stop managing spreadsheets and start managing deals.

Confident, Data-Driven Meetings

AI briefing agents surface deal context, enrichment signals, and methodology-aligned talking points before every meeting. Reps walk in prepared with a consistent value proposition grounded in data.
